Modular Burn-In Fixture

Overview

Designed a modular burn-in fixture to support parallel testing of multiple product configurations, significantly improving manufacturing throughput.

System Design

  • Modular architecture supporting all product configuration combinations
  • Custom “dummy” chassis and units for safe electrical and functional testing
  • Scalable fixture layout adaptable to changing production needs

Key Contributions

  • Designed fixture architecture for rapid reconfiguration
  • Improved burn-in capacity and test parallelism
  • Reduced production bottlenecks

Impact

Increased test throughput and flexibility while minimizing setup time and operator intervention.
