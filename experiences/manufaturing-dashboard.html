Universal Manufacturing Dashboard

Overview

Designed and deployed a comprehensive manufacturing intelligence dashboard to unify ERP, quality, and production data across the organization.

Data Sources

  • NetSuite ERP
  • Google Sheets
  • Manufacturing reporting systems

Dashboard Capabilities

  • Live Andon unit status map
  • Andon report frequency Pareto charts
  • First Pass Yield (OQC & Test)
  • Assembly productivity: person-days vs attainment
  • Quarterly build attainment by product line and sub-assembly
  • On-Time Delivery (Sales Orders & Warranty Returns)

Impact

Enabled leadership and manufacturing teams to track performance, identify risks, and make data-driven operational decisions.
