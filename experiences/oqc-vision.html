OQC AI Vision System

Problem

Manual outgoing quality checks created a production bottleneck and inconsistent inspection results. Needed a way to increase throughput while minimizing costs.

Solution

  • Custom dataset gathered & annotated in Label Studio - chose heaviest hitting assembly in quality fails.
  • Training performed on Google Colab
  • NVIDIA Xavier NX running TensorRT-optimized YOLOv8s

Impact

  • Reduced manual inspection effort, saving over $40,000 annually in labor costs on a single product line, while improving consistency.
  • Immediate operator feedback
  • Reduced reworks & inspection time by 20x
  • Establish one-peice flow in manufacturing
  • CM quality standardization
