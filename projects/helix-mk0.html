Helix Mk. 1 – Autonomous Mobile Robot

Overview

Helix Mk. 0 was an early autonomous mobile robot prototype focused on robotic mechanical design. It became a passion project for me to create my own ROS-enabled robot (leading to the current model: Helix Mk. 1), starting me on a journey since Winter of 2023 while working at Clearpath Robotics as a Robot Builder.
